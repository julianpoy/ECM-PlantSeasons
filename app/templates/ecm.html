Environmental Change-Makers of Westchester/Los Angeles

The Environmental Change-Makers community group helps people discover the skills, joys, connections, and fulfillment of the new future.
At ECM we understand that the world is changing dramatically. And we are helping people acquire the skills to cope and address world crises.
ECM offers a lively calendar of classes, projects, and events focused on the new future. Join the ECM mailing list to get news and info about events: www.EnviroChangeMakers.org
Joanne Poyourow, co-founder of ECM (and author of the material in this app) writes a blog about topics relating to sustainability. Follow Joanne at www.Change-Making.com
